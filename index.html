About Me

I am an IES Postdoctoral Fellow and an Assistant Professor (non-tenure) & Economist at the LMU & ifo Institute (currently on leave). My research interests lie in international trade, focusing on trade policy, sanctions, and welfare effects.

Research

My research focuses on international trade, particularly on the welfare effects of sanctions and evasion strategies. Key projects include:

  • Evading Trade Sanctions: Evidence from Military Goods – Published in American Economic Review.
  • The Welfare Effects of Trade Sanctions – Published in Journal of International Economics.

Data

I work with novel datasets on trade flows and enforcement mechanisms. Selected datasets are available upon request for research collaboration.
