I am an IES Postdoctoral Fellow at Princeton University and an Assistant Professor (non-tenure) and Economist at LMU Munich and the ifo Institute. I am also a CESifo and CEPR research affiliate. My research focuses on international trade and trade policy, with a particular interest in how firms and consumers adjust to changes in trade policy-induced costs.
